Buttons

Buttons are an essential way to interact with and navigate through an app, and should clearly communicate what action will occur after the user taps them. Buttons can consist of text and/or an icon, and can be enhanced with a wide variety of attributes. For accessibility reasons, buttons use a standard <button> element, but are enhanced with an ion-button directive.
